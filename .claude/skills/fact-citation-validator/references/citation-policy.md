# Citation Policy

## Goal

Every factual or numeric claim should be traceable to a source.

## Claim Examples That Need Citations

- Percentages (`37%`, `+12.4%`)
- Financial values (`$2.3M`, `¥120,000,000`)
- Volume counts (`12,400 users`, `4,500 leads`)
- Performance comparisons (`conversion improved by 18%`)

## Acceptable Citation Patterns

Strong evidence (preferred):
- `[Source Name](https://...)`
- Plain URL on the same line or nearby line

Weak evidence (allowed but warned):
- `Source: Report 2026`
- `Reference: internal survey`
- Footnote marker without URL (`[1]` only)

## Placement Rules

- Put citations on the same line as the claim when possible.
- If not possible, keep citation within 1-2 lines below the claim.
- Avoid collecting all links at the end of the slide unless each claim is explicitly mapped.

## Recommended Slide Pattern

```mdx
Market adoption reached 42% in 2025.
Source: [IDC Worldwide AI Survey 2025](https://example.com/idc-ai-2025)
```
