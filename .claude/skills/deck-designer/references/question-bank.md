# Question Bank

Structured questions for each design phase. Pick 2-3 per message.
Questions marked with * are essential; others are situational.

## Phase 1: Purpose & Audience

### Core Questions
- * What is this presentation about? (one sentence)
- * Who is the audience? (role, expertise level, relationship to you)
- * What should the audience do or think differently after watching?
- * How long is the presentation? (minutes)
- * What language? (Japanese / English)

### Context Questions
- Where will you present? (conference stage, meeting room, online, async)
- Is this a solo talk or part of a panel/series?
- Will slides be shared as a standalone document afterward?
- Is there a specific event name or date to include?

### Constraint Questions
- Are there mandatory topics you must cover?
- Are there topics you must NOT cover? (confidential, out of scope)
- Do you have existing materials (docs, blog posts, data) to incorporate?

## Phase 2: Content & Structure

### Message Discovery
- * What are the 3-5 key points you want the audience to remember?
- Is there a single "one-liner" that captures the whole talk?
- What's the most surprising or counterintuitive thing you'll share?
- Do you have data, metrics, or case studies to include?

### Structure Selection
- Does your story follow a problem-to-solution arc?
- Are you explaining a transformation (before/after)?
- Are you building a case for "why" before explaining "how"?
- Is there a natural chronological order?

### Slide Planning
- Which points need their own section divider?
- Do any points need multiple slides (deep dives)?
- Do you want a Q&A or discussion slide?
- Should there be an agenda/overview slide at the start?

### Content Depth
- Which topics should get the most slide real estate?
- Are there comparison points (us vs. them, old vs. new)?
- Do you need technical detail slides (architecture, code, config)?
- Are there quotes or testimonials to include?

## Phase 3: Design & Theme

### Brand & Identity
- * Do you have company/brand colors to use? (hex codes if known)
- * Do you have a logo file? (path or will provide later)
- Is there an existing deck style you want to match or reference?
- Any corporate style guide constraints?

### Visual Direction
- What mood should the deck convey? (professional, energetic, minimal, bold, warm, techy)
- Light background or dark background preference?
- Do you prefer clean/minimal or information-dense slides?
- Any design styles you like? (describe or reference existing decks)

### Typography
- Do you have font preferences? (serif, sans-serif, specific fonts)
- For Japanese content: Noto Sans JP is default. Prefer something else?

### Layout Preferences
- Copyright text to display? (e.g., "© 2026 CORe Inc.")
- Logo position preference? (top-left, top-right, bottom-left, etc.)
- Want an accent line on the side? (left/right gradient stripe)
- Page number position? (bottom-right is default)
